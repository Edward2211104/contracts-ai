# Scope (v0.1)
Families: lease, insurance, services, telecom

Canonical questions (examples):
- Can I terminate early without penalty?
- Who pays for water damage?
- What is the cure period and notice requirement?
- Is there an auto-renewal? How to prevent it?
- Limitation of liability cap?
- Governing law and dispute venue? Arbitration?

Outputs must always include citations to exact contract spans (page, clause_id).
